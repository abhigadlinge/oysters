training flow and content

The program will be a mix of activities, brainstorming’s and trainer led delivery for 4 hours. The training program will engage participants with various activities such as Q&A, Individual and group activities, presentations, discussions, etc.

Negotiation Strategy & PI Activity:

  • Identifying different negotiation styles and their strengths and weaknesses.
  • Learning the key principles of negotiation, such as preparation, active listening, and compromise.
  • Practicing negotiation skills through role-playing exercises and case studies
  • Identifying common negotiation mistakes and how to avoid them.
  • Developing negotiation strategies that align with company goals and values.

Value & Pursued Value & Activity :

  • Creating a positive first impression
  • Establishing common ground
  • Highlighting the benefits of working for the organization
  • Providing concrete examples Addressing any concerns or objections
