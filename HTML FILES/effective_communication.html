effective communication

“Communication is the solvent of all problems and is the foundation for personal development.”

get a quote

Communication Apprehension Test (Conduct pre session or in session )

Ist Half (2 Hours)

  1. Introduction & Ice Breaking (10-20 mins)
  2. Objective & Context Setting of the Session
  3. Trainers & Participants Introduction

The 8Cs of Effective Communication (20 mins)

Note: Can also be shared as a pre read material and all these 7C’s can be brainstormed and explained with the help of activities and discussions in the session.

Clear Correct Complete Concrete Concise Considered Courteous Collaborative Comm. Find examples for both Anti and in support of all 7C’s

Activities to Improve Effective Communication in a team (30-45 mins) 

            Human Knot
            Back to Back Drawing
            The Minefield
            Chinese Whispers (with all versions)

Effective Body Language leads to Effective Communication (30-45 mins)

  • Activity- Give 60-90 secs to each participant & give them different- different sets of topics & scenarios and ask them to enact without speaking and then note and observe their body language
  • These 60-90 sec shots can also be recorded and shown to the participants at the end of the session during feedback & Conclusion session for their improvements & inputs.
  • Role Play Activity & Debrief with participants

IInd Half (2 Hours)

Barriers To Communication (Activity + Brainstorming) (45-60 mins)

Brainstorming with Participants (45-60 mins) Followed by a Brainstorming Activity Based on all the activities & Learning from the First half of the sessions,now we will let the participants ideate & discuss on how they believe effective communications can be achieved. Getting to some practical problems or challenges faced by them and trying to get to the root of it and try to come up with some solutions.

Please Note: We sincerely advise no senior level employee/boss to be present in the session room(Until unless they are participants themselves). So that the participants can freely express themselves & speak their minds.

The Final Act (30-40 mins)

7 Principles of Positive Collaborative Communications(Examples with activities)

Divide all the participants in a group of 5-6 people and a hypothetical scenario will be created based on a real type of work problem, provided with data,facts & situations. Participants will be designated with various roles and will have to act accordingly. And as a group it will be observed how effectively you all communicated with each other and how you addressed the challenge.

Right Listening Activity Conclusion, Q&A and Feedback Session (15-20 mins)

10 strategies to help you communicate more effectively

21 days of effective communication(can be used very actively as a Post Training Self Development Activities(PTSD’s) for the participants)
